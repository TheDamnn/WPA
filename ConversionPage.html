Measurement conversion is the process of changing the units of a quantity from one system to another. It is a common task in various fields, such as science, engineering, cooking, and everyday life. The purpose of measurement conversion is to express a quantity in a different unit of measurement while maintaining the same value.

Centimeter(cm) Inches(inch)
A centimeter is a metric unit for the measurement of length of objects and small distances. It is written using the symbol cm. It can also be defined as the unit of length in the International System of Units (SI), the current form of the metric system. It is equivalent to 1/100 meters. Inch explained in simple words is the customary unit of length that is one-twelfth of a foot, widely used in the United States for measuring small distances. Inch symbol is given by the double prime or double quotes (″).
Centimeters are convenient for measurements in scientific research, education, and everyday use, especially when dealing with metric units. Inches are sometimes more convenient for measuring smaller objects or dimensions, especially in fields like carpentry or construction.
1 cm = 0.39 inch 1 inch = 2.54 cm
